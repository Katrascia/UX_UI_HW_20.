    MyGroup

    As many countries have lifted travel restrictions, tourism is back with full force and influence. The process of traveling as a group is a unique experience that is both fulfilling, for some, and tedious for others. I sought to understand the thought process of travelers while planning group travel and examine their experiences.

    User Research:

    Key Insight #1

    People love to travel with others because they love the companionship.

    Key Insight #2

    People find that planning group trips can be overwhelming and stressful.

    Key Insight #3

    People love the convenience of hotels but prefer airbnb for group travel for more space.

    Problem Statement

    Chrissy wants to travel with her family. Chrissy finds it overwhelming dealing with everyone and keeping track of all the information (reservations, schedules, excursions, etc.).
